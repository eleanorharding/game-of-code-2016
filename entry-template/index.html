Can I _____?

Brought to you by Eleanor Harding and Anchal Bassi

The idea in a nutshell:

  1. The internet is filled with conflicting information
  2. Finding the straightforward answer to a health question is a nightmare
  3. We want to simplify this process

This app will provide clear, vetted Go/No-Go answers to health questions in specific niche areas.

I'm _____,
can I _____?

I'm pregnant,
can I do pushups?

I'm diabetic,
can I drink alcohol?

I'm allergic to penicillin,
can I eat blue cheese?

In an online world filled with conflicting advice, I want to ask a question and get a clear answer quickly.

The Mission:

To create and curate a trusted, easy to use database of correct Go/No-Go answers to the world's health-related questions.

Growth:

Targeting a range of hyperniche communities will ensure scalable long term growth

Partnerships:

This app will leverage partnerships with domain experts to sponsor answers. For example, the pregnancy questions could be sponsored by fitpregnancy.com, where they would pay a fee to vouch for the answer and have their site visible as an authoratative link on the answer page

The App:

The MVP will be a web-app built on Node.js. We'll start by building out the database and building tools to scrape content for the database to be used in the webapp. We will then move on to native iOS as a complementary outlet.

Why it will work:

  1. This project is addressing a real need through a simple, elegant solution.
  2. We have the skills needed to take this project from zero to one
